Section 03

GPS and Location Data

Because the Service is a public-transport routing application, location data is central to our functionality. We want to be as transparent as possible about how we handle it.

3.1 When Location is Accessed

3ala Gamb accesses your GPS location only when the application is open in the foreground. Your location is never tracked in the background and never when the application is closed.

3.2 What We Do with Your Location

  • Displaying the map: Your current position is shown on the map and used to centre the view.
  • Defaulting the route origin: Your current position is pre-filled as the origin for a new route search.
  • Computing routes: The origin and destination coordinates of each route search are sent to our backend to compute public-transport routes.
  • Storing favourite locations: If you choose to save a favourite location (Home, Work, or Preferred), its coordinates and name are stored in your Account.

3.3 How to Revoke Location Permission

You may revoke location permission at any time through your device's operating-system settings:

  • Android: Settings › Apps › 3ala Gamb › Permissions › Location
  • iOS: Settings › Privacy & Security › Location Services › 3ala Gamb

If you revoke location permission, the Service will continue to function but will require you to manually enter or select the origin of each route.

Section 04

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected.

Data Category Retention Period
Account data (name, email, hashed password) For as long as your Account is active, then deleted immediately upon account deletion
Profile picture For as long as you choose to keep it uploaded, then deleted immediately upon removal or account deletion
Favourite locations For as long as your Account is active, then deleted immediately upon account deletion

4.1 Anonymisation on Account Deletion

When you delete your Account, we proceed by immediately and permanently deleting all personal identifiers — name, email, hashed password, profile photo, and favourite locations — in compliance with your right to erasure.

Section 06

Security of Your Personal Data

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, and damage:

  • Encryption in transit: All communications between the application and our backend use HTTPS/TLS.
  • Encryption at rest: Databases and file storage on our backend are encrypted at rest.
  • Access controls: Access to production systems is restricted on a need-to-know basis and requires strong authentication.
  • Password hashing: User passwords are stored using industry-standard hashing algorithms and are never stored in plain text.

Despite these measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a personal data breach likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and the competent supervisory authority within the timeframes required by applicable law.

Section 07

Account Deletion

You may delete your Account at any time by either:

  1. Using the in-application account deletion function: Profile › Delete Account
  2. Sending a written request to info@3alagamb.com
Account deletion is immediate and permanent. Your name, email, hashed password, profile photograph, and favourite locations are permanently deleted from our active systems. You may continue to use the Service as a Guest User if you wish.
